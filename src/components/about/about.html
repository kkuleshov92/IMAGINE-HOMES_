A LITTLE BIT ABOUT US

Amy Courage

Courage & Co is a boutique architecture and interior design firm run by Amy L. Courage, AIA ASID, based in Riverside, CT. Amy’s career has spanned over 3 decades, founding the architecture firm of Lesser Fifield in 1982 and Lesser & Frisch, Architects in 1986, both in NYC, subsequently running her own offices in Charlottesville, Chicago and Connecticut.

She has designed some of the more significant renovation and restoration projects in New York, including the Puck Building, Alwyn Court and the Carlyle Hotel, but has returned to her greatest passion – residential architecture and interiors.

A signature home by Courage & Co expresses elegance, vitality & comfort, balancing style with a sensible regard for how people live and work in space. Rooted in classicism but with a light whimsical touch, Amy’s work always nods to tradition with a keen eye to the future.

Paul Kramer

Paul started his company in 1976 as a carpenter In Croton-on-Hudson NY and by 1985 grew to employ carpenters, masons and earth workers to provide a complete in-house construction company working in Fairfield and Westchester Counties. His building experience includes major home remodels, new custom homes and spec homes.

With 44 years of broad, hands-on construction experience, Paul can provide a unique level of construction knowledge which translates to a sound financial cornerstone for those considering home remodeling.
